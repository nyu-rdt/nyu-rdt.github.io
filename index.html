WHO WE ARE

NYU Robotic Design Team(RDT) is a student-led engineering team composed of highly motivated individuals with cross-disciplinary experiences. Our goal is to create, design, and implement innovative robots that contribute to advancement in the field while positively impacting our school and community. We are annual participants of the NASA Lunabotics Competition and have achieved various recognitions in the previous years.

VIP INFORMATION

NYU Robotic Design Team is one of the Vertically Integrated Projects at NYU Tandon school of engineering. This project focuses on community outreach, project management, system engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical/computer engineering, and software engineering. The project is advised by Guiseppe Loianno.
