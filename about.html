WHO WE ARE
We design and build an extraterrestrial mining robot for the NASA Lunabotics Competition.
We build a new robot yearly to compete in NASA's Lunabotics challenge.
OUR MISSION
Our mission is to create innovative robots for the advancement of the field, while having a positive impact on our school and community.
The Competition
NASA's Lunabotics Competition is a lunar mining competition to promote research in lunar excavation. We compete against other colleges in person each year.
